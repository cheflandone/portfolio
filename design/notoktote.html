NOT OK: A TOTE PROJECT

This piece is a personal exploration of mental health, using design as both a canvas and a conversation. Through this piece, I wanted to communicate emotions that are often difficult to express in words, using design as a bridge to connect on a more intimate level.



This project was really special to me and it was important for me to make it. My best friend Paige, encouraged me to see the whole thing through and get the final product made. I don't currently have access to screen printing so I ordered this through a third party printing service from Oslo, Norway. Somewhere I have a strong fascination with.

If you want one, let me know :)
