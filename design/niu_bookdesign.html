Northern Illinois University: Book Cover Design

At Northern Illinois University in Graphic Communications I, this project was making five different book covers for a made up client. We had to make patterns, use the patterns, use our own photographic material, and vector shapes that were provided to us. We were also restricted to the Univers font family. I had a wonderful professor, she was really honest in the critiques and I feel she helped me become a better designer in countless ways.
