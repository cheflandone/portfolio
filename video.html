SUPERCUT: A VIDEO MONTAGE CREATED TO DISPLAY MULTIPLE EVENTS OF SIGNIFICANCE OR OF IMPORTANCE. OFTEN WITHIN A SET TIMELINE. CREATING A WHOLE PIECE, TO COMMUNICATE AN IDEA.

I’ve been making these for years. But in 2021 my world imploded, as it does for many 19 year olds. This led to a very intense and eventful year in 2022 which prompted me to take this form of documentary and not just make it a corny montage, but turn it into an art piece itself. Playing with composition and building a cinematic experience that captures what it really is to be ‘coming of age’ in the 21st century.

As someone who is really dorky with technical stuff like video encoding and audio mixing I use this as an exercise to practice and perfect the quality of these short films.

This is my passion project, my thesis, and my legacy. I want to take these memories and present it how I feel it. A glorious kaleidoscope of love, liberation, and the fear that comes with growing into yourself.

In 2023, I spent the entire year intently recording moments + b-roll like scenery to build a more cohesive narrative + setting. I divided it up into chapters signifying important moments in my life.
For example, a few were things like, moving to a very queer neighborhood, beginning a relationship, and ending the relationship when I realized I had to stand up for myself, the liberation that followed, a difficult semester in school that led to serious burnout. These were things that mean a lot to me on a personal level.
